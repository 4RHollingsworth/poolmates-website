"What's Included?"

Each opening option includes the following services:

  • Cover Removal (cleaned, folded and prepped for summer storage)
  • Power Washing of Deck
  • Re-Installing deck and pool equipment
  • Initial Vacuum
  • Initial Chemical Balancing
  • Start up of the pump and filter
  • Heater can be cleaned and lit at a reduced rate if booked with the opening
  • Each opening includes 1 vacuum and up to 2 visits only.
  • Excessively leaf ridden pools, or pools that require more than 2 visits will be charged additionally

    • Please do not use robotic pool cleaners during the opening process as they tend to stir up debris and cause extra visits
