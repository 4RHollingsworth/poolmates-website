Pool Openings

It's that time of year to open the pool up...

"What's Included?"

Our pool opening service includes the following steps to ensure your pool is ready for the season:

  • Removal and cleaning of your pool cover
  • Reconnecting your equipment like pumps, filters, and heaters
  • Testing and balancing water chemistry
  • Initial vacuum and debris removal
  • Inspecting pool equipment for any damage

What You Need to Know Before We Arrive

Before our team comes to open your pool, please make sure to:

  • Ensure your water level is at least halfway up the skimmer
  • Clear any large debris from the pool cover
  • Turn off any automatic timers or pool covers
  • Ensure easy access to your pool equipment
  • Let us know about any concerns or repairs needed
