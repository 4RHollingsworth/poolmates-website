Before You Call

Prior to calling us about a safety cover, please:

  • Have a rough measurement of your pool -- Dimensions & Shape
  • Ensure at least 24" or 60cm of deck space around the perimeter of your pool*

* It is ideal to have 3 feet (36‚Äù) of decking around the perimeter of the pool, but it is not required

All covers are quoted based on a site visit and no pricing is confirmed until you receive a proper written quote from us

"What's Included?"

Safety Cover Installations conducted by Pool Mates includes:

  • Professional measurement of your pool to ensure fit, as each cover is custom fit to the pool
  • Installation of the cover with all the correct hardware to ensure proper fit and support (hardware based on type of deck, amount of space for the anchors/springs to be installed) etc.
  • Recommended to have measured, ordered and installed during July and August so that your cover is ready for closing season. We will install it, and take it back off the pool for you to continue swimming

Not Included

Pool Mates' Safety Cover Installations do NOT include:

  • Closing of your pool

What You Need to Know Before We Arrive

Before our team comes to install your safety cover, please make sure of the following:

  • Access to the yard (Gate codes, or key locations -- To be given at time of booking)
  • Clear access to equipment
  • Backyard is clear of any pet waste (Please Poop n' Scoop!)
  • Ensure water and electricity are accessible
  • Any parts relevant to pool need to be left out
    (Salt cell, baskets, drain plugs, winter plugs, winter covers, etc.)

Important Notes:

  • Unfortunately we are unable to call prior to our arrival. We will arrive on the date that your call has been scheduled. Pool Mates call you only if issues arise
  • If we do not have access on the scheduled day or if the pool is not prepared for service, your appointment will be rescheduled at our earliest convenience, and a $75.00 + HST surcharge will apply
