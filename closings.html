CLOSINGS

Prepare your pool for a restful winter with our comprehensive closing service!
We handle everything to ensure your pool is securely winterized, so you can enjoy peace of mind and look forward to next swim season!

Before You Call

Prior to calling us about closing your pool, please note:

  • It is HIGHLY recommended that you book as early as possible in order to ensure you get as close to the date as you like
  • We can take bookings for other years at any time, so feel free to book ahead

Consider booking your opening date when you book your closing

"What's Included?"

Pool closings conducted by Pool Mates include:

  • Basic vacuum of your pool
  • Lowering of the pool water
  • Uninstalling deck equipment if applicable
  • Winterization of lines and equipment
  • Balancing of water if necessary *
  • Adding of antifreeze and algaecide *
  • Cleaning of salt cell *
  • Installation of winter cover

Green pools cannot be cleaned at closing -- They must be cleaned prior to our arrival

* Extra charges may apply

What You Need to Know Before We Arrive

Before our team comes to close your pool, please make sure of the following:

  • Access to the yard (Gate codes, or key locations -- To be given at time of booking)
  • Clear access to equipment
  • Backyard is clear of any pet waste (Please Poop n' Scoop!)
  • Ensure water and electricity are accessible
  • Any parts relevant to pool need to be left out
    (Salt cell, baskets, drain plugs, winter plugs, winter covers, etc.)

Important Notes:

  • Unfortunately we are unable to call prior to our arrival. We will arrive on the date that your call has been scheduled. Pool Mates call you only if issues arise
  • If we do not have access on the scheduled day or if the pool is not prepared for service, your appointment will be rescheduled at our earliest convenience, and a $75.00 + HST surcharge will apply
