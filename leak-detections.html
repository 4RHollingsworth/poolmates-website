LEAK DETECTIONS

Is your pool losing water? Our expert leak detection services can help pinpoint and resolve the issue!

Before You Call

Prior to calling us about a leak, please perform the following 3 tests:

Place a piece of tape halfway up the skimmer and fill the pool so the water is at the base of the tape.

Before each step, ensure the water level is at the original starting point for proper comparison.

  • Run your pool for 24 hours with the pump on. Measure any water loss in inches.
  • Run your pool for 24 hours with the pump off. Measure any water loss in inches.
  • Plug your pool skimmer and jets using winter plugs (do not lower the water level) and leave your pump off for 24 hours. Measure loss in inches. This last test is to determine if the leak is in the shell of the pool.

Please measure the results in inches and use photographic evidence to back it up (before and after pictures of each test). You can text the results to 416-282-0999

"What's Included?"

Based on your findings, our team will tailor the detection process to identify the leak efficiently:

  • Pressure Testing: We inspect the plumbing lines using specialized equipment to detect leaks in underground or hidden pipework.
  • Shell Inspection: If the leak appears to be in the pool's structure, we may deploy a certified diver in a wetsuit to inspect the pool shell for cracks, damage, or weak spots.

*Please note that our leak detections only tend of find the largest leak in your pool*

What You Need to Know Before We Arrive

Before our team comes to conduct a leak detection, please make sure of the following:

  • Access to the yard (Gate codes, or key locations -- To be given at time of booking)
  • Clear access to equipment
  • Animal feces has been scooped up
  • Water and electricity access
  • Any parts relevant to pool need to be left out
    (Salt cell, backets, drain plugs, winter plugs, winter covers, etc.)

We DO NOT call prior to our arrival. We will be there on the scheduled date and if any issues arise, we will call you.

If we DO NOT have access on the scheduled day, or if the pool is not ready for us to service, the appointment will be rescheduled at OUR earliest convenience and a $75.00 + HST surgarge will apply
