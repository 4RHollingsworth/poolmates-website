LEAK DETECTIONS

Before You Call

To help us better understand the source of your pool's leak, please complete the following 3 tests and document your results:

Step 1: Place a piece of tape halfway up the skimmer and fill the pool so the water is at the base of the tape

Step 2: Before each test, ensure the water level is at the original starting point for accurate comparison

  • Run your pool for 24 hours with the pump on. Use a ruler to measure water loss in inches
  • Leave your pool off for 24 hours with the pump off. Again, measure the water loss in inches
  • Plug your pool skimmer and jets using winter plugs, without lowering the water level, and keep the pump off for 24 hours. Measure the water loss in inches
    (This step determines if the leak is in the pool's shell.)

Please submit your results (in inches) along with before and after photos of each test by texting them to (416) 282-0999.

"What's Included?"

Based on your findings, our team will use specialized techniques to identify the source of the leak:

  • Pressure Testing: We inspect your pool’s plumbing lines using advanced equipment to locate leaks in underground or hidden pipework.
  • Shell Inspection: If the leak is suspected to be in the pool’s structure, we may use a certified diver to perform a hands-on inspection of the pool shell for cracks, damage, or weak spots.

*Please note: Our leak detections are designed to identify the most significant leaks in your pool first. Secondary or minor leaks may require additional investigation.*

What You Need to Know Before We Arrive

To ensure a smooth and efficient leak detection process, please ensure the following before our arrival:

  • Access to the yard (Gate codes, or key locations -- To be given at time of booking)
  • Clear access to equipment
  • Backyard is clear of any pet waste (Please Poop n' Scoop!)
  • Ensure water and electricity are accessible
  • Any parts relevant to pool need to be left out
    (Salt cell, baskets, drain plugs, winter plugs, winter covers, etc.)

Important Notes:

  • Unfortunately we are unable to call prior to our arrival. We will arrive on the date that your call has been scheduled. Pool Mates call you only if issues arise
  • If we do not have access on the scheduled day or if the pool is not prepared for service, your appointment will be rescheduled at our earliest convenience, and a $75.00 + HST surcharge will apply
