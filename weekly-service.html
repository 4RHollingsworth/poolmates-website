"What's Included?"

Weekly services conducted by Pool Mates include:

  • Skimming and cleaning of pool surface
  • Vacuuming of pool
  • Testing chemistry of pool and necessary balancing
  • Refilling of automatic chlorine puck feeders where applicable
  • Emptying of skimmer and pump baskets
  • Backwashing of filter if necessary

NOTE: This service is not meant for excessively algae filled or leaf ridden pools.
Multiple trips and chemicals will likely be needed in these secific situations.
