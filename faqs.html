Please note that chlorine tested directly from the take-home container will show as white on test strips because the concentration is significantly higher than what standard test strips are designed to measure. This will change once it mixes with water in your pool. If your chlorine level was at zero, the chlorine will enter the pool, do its job, and burn out, potentially leaving no residual chlorine after that dosage.
