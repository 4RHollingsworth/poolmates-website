EQUIPMENT REPAIR

Pool Mates service professionals help you choose the proper equipment, professionally installed, to ensure a smooth summer.

Before You Call

Prior to calling us about repairing or installing equipment, please:

Gather Essential Information

  • Photographs of your current equipment (to send ahead of time for accurate assistance)
  • Pump motor size (e.g., 1/2 HP, 3/4 HP, 1 HP, etc.)
  • Filter Make and model number (usually found on the label)
  • BTU rating of the existing heater (found inside the heater door)
  • Approximate pool dimensions (e.g., 16x32)

    • Contact Methods

    Send photos to 416-282-0999 for the Kingston Road location (via text)
    Email photos to info@poolmates.ca for the Railside location

"What's Included?"

Equipment Repairs and Installations by Pool Mates include:

  • Heater installs, hookups, and troubleshooting by TSSA-licensed gas technicians
  • Sand or cartridge filter installation and calibration
  • Salt system installation and maintenance
  • Pump installation, repair, and optimization for efficiency
  • Complete replumbing of equipment pads for easier future maintenance and improved performance

What You Need to Know Before We Arrive

Before our team comes to repair or install equipment, please ensure the following:

  • Access to the yard (Gate codes, or key locations -- To be given at time of booking)
  • Clear access to equipment
  • Backyard is clear of any pet waste (Please Poop n' Scoop!)
  • Ensure water and electricity are accessible
  • Any parts relevant to pool need to be left out
    (Salt cell, baskets, drain plugs, winter plugs, winter covers, etc.)

Important Notes:

  • Unfortunately we are unable to call prior to our arrival. We will arrive on the date that your call has been scheduled. Pool Mates call you only if issues arise
  • If we do not have access on the scheduled day or if the pool is not prepared for service, your appointment will be rescheduled at our earliest convenience, and a $75.00 + HST surcharge will apply
